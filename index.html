The Game of War!

It is quite easy to play! For ease, a deck of 52 cards has been shuffled randomly and 26 cards have been dealt to you (Player One) and your opponent (Player Two) each.

Simply click the "Draw Card!" button and a card will be drawn from the top of your hand. Each player turns up a card at the same time and the player with the higher card takes both cards and they are placed on the bottom of your hand.

If the cards are the same rank, it is War! Each player turns up one card face down and one card face up. The player with the higher cards takes both piles (six cards). If the turned-up cards are again the same rank, each player places another card face down and turns another card face up. The player with the higher card takes all 10 cards, and so on!
